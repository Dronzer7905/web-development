About Me

I'm Ansh Gautam, a Full-Stack Developer and Entrepreneur from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University in 2025.

I founded DevTailored — a bootstrapped software studio building digital products for schools, coaching centers, and small businesses across India. Our products include Vidya Tools (school ERP), Online Dukan (WhatsApp-based ordering), MVP Lab (rapid prototyping), and ProBrand (portfolio websites).

I also build open-source tools — most notably Snapfix, a VS Code extension that catches Python errors in real time and suggests AI-powered fixes. I believe in building fast, shipping early, and solving real problems.
